# About Me

## Role & Responsibilities
- Founder / decision-maker at Trinade AI Technologies Private Limited
- Building the company's corporate website — design-first, Awwwards.Com-quality
- Not a UI/UX designer by training, but has a sharp eye for exceptional web design
- Can clearly distinguish between a fantastic site and an average one
- Success looks like: a site worthy of submission to Awwwards.com

## Design Sensibility
- **What I love**: Awwwards-winning sites — bold typography, smooth scroll-driven animations, intentional whitespace, glassmorphism, cinematic hero sections
- **What I hate**: Generic AI company websites, cookie-cutter templates, default-looking pages, anything that feels "average"
- **How I work**: I provide visual references (screenshots, URLs) and iterate by feel. I know what looks right when I see it.
- **Inspiration sites used so far**:
  - IntegratedBio — hero section reference (original starting point)
  - Joby Aviation — logo/brand treatment reference
  - Datawizz — footer design reference (giant brand text, atmospheric layers)
  - Qatalog — navbar layout reference (logo independent from pill, pill contains nav + CTA)
  - slothui — contact form reference (dark glassmorphic form inputs, split layout)

## Domain Context
- **Company**: Trinade AI Technologies Private Limited
- **Industry**: AI-first company that also provides other services
- **Brand symbol**: Trinity concept — 3 overlapping human silhouettes, representing collaboration/intelligence
- **Brand accent**: Teal (#00d4aa) primary, lime (#c8e64e) secondary, amber (#b48237) tertiary. The finalized colors are yet to be decided.
- **Logo**: Original Trinade logo — 3 overlapping head profiles with neural mesh, converted to transparent PNG. Logo variations (5 SVGs in `public/logo-variations/`) exist but final hasn't been picked yet.
- **Name origin**: "Trinade" relates to Trinity (3)
- **Typography**: Manrope — chosen as the single unified font family after exploring 10 options (Sora, Syne, Cormorant Garamond, Playfair Display, etc.)
