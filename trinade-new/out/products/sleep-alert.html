How It Works
Intelligentmonitoring,instantresponse
01

Detect

An in-cabin camera identifies the driver's face and locks onto eye position in real time.

02

Track

The system continuously monitors eye openness, measuring blink patterns and closure duration.

03

Alert

When eye-closure crosses a configurable threshold, an audible alarm activates instantly.

04

Resume

Once eyes reopen, the alert stops and seamless background monitoring continues throughout the trip.
