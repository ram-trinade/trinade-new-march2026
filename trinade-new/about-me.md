# About Me

## Role & Responsibilities
- Founder / decision-maker at Trinade AI Technologies Private Limited
- Building the company's corporate website — design-first, Awwwards.com-quality
- Not a UI/UX designer by training, but has a sharp eye for exceptional web design
- Can clearly distinguish between a fantastic site and an average one
- Success looks like: a site worthy of submission to Awwwards.com

## Design Sensibility
- **What I love**: Awwwards-winning sites — bold typography, smooth scroll-driven animations, intentional whitespace, glassmorphism, cinematic hero sections
- **What I hate**: Generic AI company websites, cookie-cutter templates, default-looking pages, anything that feels "average"
- **How I work**: I provide visual references (screenshots, URLs) and iterate by feel. I know what looks right when I see it.
- **Inspiration sites used for this website**:
  - Harkcap — sliding info cards, left nav + expanding panel
  - Aventura Dental Arts — luxurious backgrounds, atmospheric dark sections
  - IT Solutions Inc — page structure, hero, industry cards, accordion services
  - WA Solutions — diagonal section transitions, glassmorphic cards
  - Richtech Robotics — credibility grid, light+dark split layouts
  - Fourmula — navbar inspiration (menu pill + scroll percentage)
