## Design Qualities I Value
- Bold, oversized typography that commands attention
- Smooth scroll-driven animations that reveal content cinematically
- Intentional whitespace — elements that breathe
- Glassmorphism done right (not overdone)
- Dark/light section alternation for visual rhythm
- Atmospheric backgrounds (gradients, grain, depth)
- Premium feel — would look at home on Awwwards.com
